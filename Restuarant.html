About us

Discover Our Restaurant Story

Food is wonderful. It engages all our senses. It plays several roles in our lives. It brings people together. It sustains us and comforts us. It’s the center of various of our celebrations. Many of us show our love through the food we cook and feel loved when someone cooks us our favorite meal. We all love food, and we all need food. Unfortunately, 795 million people – 1 out of every 9 people worldwide – do not have enough to eat. Nearly 20 million children are severely malnourished. We can change this. We believe a hunger free world is possible – a world where everyone has enough food for today and for tomorrow.

One of the best joys in life is getting to eat delicious food! Some even travel to different countries just to get a taste of their authentic cuisine and unique dishes
